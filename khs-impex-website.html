Who We Are

About KHS Impex

Export-oriented manufacturing excellence based in Noida, India, serving international buyers with commitment to quality and consistency.

KHS Impex Pvt. Ltd. is an export-oriented manufacturing company specializing in knitted home furnishing products, knitted apparel, and knitted shoe uppers. With over 25 years of industry experience, we are based in Noida, India, and serve international buyers with a strong commitment to quality compliance, consistent production, and long-term partnerships.

With extensive experience in handling buyer-specific requirements, we focus on manufacturing products that meet global quality standards, offering flexibility in design, materials, and packaging to suit diverse export markets.

Innovation

Advanced Technology

Cutting-edge Flyknit technology for superior footwear solutions

Seamless Construction

Our advanced Flyknit technology eliminates the need for excessive stitching, creating seamless uppers that enhance flexibility, comfort, and overall shoe performance.

  • Enhanced flexibility
  • Superior comfort
  • Reduced production waste
  • Better breathability

Precision Engineering

Different knit zones engineered for specific purposes - breathability where needed, flexibility in movement areas, and reinforcement in high-stress zones.

  • Zone-specific performance
  • Optimized breathability
  • Targeted reinforcement
  • Custom flexibility

Sustainable Manufacturing

Committed to environmental responsibility through zero-waste knitting processes and eco-friendly material options including GRS-certified recycled polyester and organic cotton.

  • Zero-waste production
  • Recycled materials
  • GRS certification available
  • Reduced carbon footprint
